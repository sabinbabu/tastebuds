To make bacon strips and melted cheese-topped fries, start by preparing your fries using your preferred method. You can use frozen fries and bake them according to the package instructions, usually at around 425°F (220°C) for 20-25 minutes, or cook them in an air fryer at 400°F (200°C) for 15-20 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through.

Cooked rice with vegetable salad

16-06-2024

To make a delicious cooked rice with vegetable salad, start by preparing 1 cup of rice according to package instructions, usually by rinsing the rice under cold water, then cooking it in 2 cups of water brought to a boil. Once the water boils, reduce the heat, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until the rice is tender and the water is absorbed.

Donuts

06-06-2024

To make homemade donuts, begin by mixing 2 1/4 teaspoons of active dry yeast with 1/4 cup of warm water and a pinch of sugar, letting it sit for about 5-10 minutes until frothy. In a separate large bowl, combine 3/4 cup of warm milk, 1/4 cup of granulated sugar, 2 tablespoons of melted butter, 1 teaspoon of salt, and 2 eggs.

Fruit Volcano

To make a Fruit Volcano, begin by selecting a variety of fresh fruits such as pineapples, strawberries, kiwis, blueberries, and grapes. Cut the fruits into bite-sized pieces, making sure to keep a balance of colors and flavors. Take a large, round fruit, such as a watermelon or a pineapple, and cut off the top third, hollowing out the inside to create a bowl. Reserve the scooped-out fruit for later use. Place the hollowed fruit on a large serving platter.

Pancakes with fruits

To make pancakes with fruits, start by preparing the pancake batter. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together 1 cup of all-purpose flour, 2 tablespoons of sugar, 1 teaspoon of baking powder, and a pinch of salt. In another bowl, whisk together 1 cup of milk, 1 egg, and 2 tablespoons of melted butter.

Popcorn kernels

To make popcorn from kernels, begin by heating a large, heavy-bottomed pot over medium-high heat and adding 2-3 tablespoons of oil with a high smoke point, such as vegetable, canola, or coconut oil. Place three or four popcorn kernels into the oil and cover the pot. When these test kernels pop, add the remaining 1/3 cup of popcorn kernels in an even layer.

Three noodle dishes with orange sauces

To create three noodle dishes with orange sauces, start with a classic Orange Chicken Stir-Fry with Noodles. Begin by cooking your choice of noodles (like rice noodles or lo mein) according to the package instructions. While the noodles cook, prepare the orange sauce by combining orange juice, soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, garlic, ginger, and a bit of cornstarch to thicken.
